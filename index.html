Profile

A career
built across
continents

From managing retail banking on Vancouver's Chinatown corridors to pioneering consumer brands across 15 Asia-Pacific markets — Tony Tse has spent four decades at the intersection of commerce, culture, and innovation.

A graduate of UBC's Sauder School of Business (Dean's Honour List), Tony has held senior leadership roles at Royal Bank of Canada, Faber-Castell, Philips, WestPoint Stevens, DuPont/Invista, and Advansa. His career has been defined by a rare ability to bridge Western brands with Asian markets — building supply chains, launching flagship stores, and commercialising breakthrough technologies across China, Japan, Australia, and beyond.

Today, Tony is the Founder and CEO of Major 7 Global Sustainability Co. Ltd., partnering with Plastic Bank Canada on a mission to recover ocean-bound plastic — with 10 billion bottles collected worldwide.
