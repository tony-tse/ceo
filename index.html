Profile

A career built
across continents

From managing retail banking on Vancouver's Chinatown corridors to pioneering consumer brands across 15 Asia-Pacific markets — Tony Tse has spent four decades at the intersection of commerce, culture, and innovation.

A graduate of UBC's Sauder School of Business (Dean's Honour List, 1987), Tony has held senior leadership roles at Royal Bank of Canada, Faber-Castell, Philips, WestPoint Stevens, DuPont/Invista, and Advansa. His career has been defined by a rare ability to bridge Western brands with Asian markets — building supply chains, launching flagship stores, and commercialising breakthrough technologies across China, Japan, Australia, and beyond.

Today, Tony is the Founder and CEO of Major 7 Global Sustainability Co. Ltd., providing ESG strategy and sustainable materials consulting for global brands operating in Asia-Pacific. He is EcoVadis Silver Certified and aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
